## Daily Workflow

### Morning (8:00 AM - Automated)
```
1. Search trending funny clips (YouTube, Twitter, Reddit)
2. Download top 3 candidates
3. Transcribe audio
4. Translate to 10 languages
5. Generate dubbed audio
6. Add subtitles
7. Queue for posting
```

### Afternoon (Manual Review)
```
1. Review queued videos
2. Upload to YouTube (long-form compilations)
3. Upload to TikTok/YouTube Shorts (short clips)
4. Upload to Bilibili/Douyin (Chinese versions)
5. Monitor engagement
```

### Weekly
```
1. Compile best clips into long-form videos
2. Analyze what's working (views, engagement)
3. Adjust strategy based on data
4. Process any revenue
```

## Content Strategy

### Short-Form (TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels)
- **Duration:** 30-60 seconds
- **Format:** Vertical (1080×1920)
- **Frequency:** 3-5 per day per platform
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds must grab attention
- **CTA:** "Follow for more!" or "Full video on YouTube"

### Long-Form (YouTube)
- **Duration:** 8-15 minutes
- **Format:** Landscape (1920×1080)
- **Frequency:** 2-3 per week
- **Content:** Compilations of best short clips
- **Monetization:** Mid-roll ads at 8+ minutes

### Chinese Market (Bilibili, Douyin)
- **Duration:** 30-120 seconds
- **Format:** Vertical for Douyin, landscape for Bilibili
- **Frequency:** 2-3 per day
- **Content:** Chinese-dubbed versions with subtitles
- **Advantage:** Less competition, huge audience

## Risk Mitigation

### Copyright
- Only use content we have rights to (public domain, fair use)
- Add commentary/analysis to make it transformative
- Credit original creators
- Don't compete with originals

### Platform Rules
- Follow each platform's community guidelines
- Don't spam (post quality over quantity)
- Engage with audience
- Don't use copyrighted music

### Technical
- Backup all content
- Monitor for platform changes
- Diversify across platforms
- Don't rely on single revenue source
